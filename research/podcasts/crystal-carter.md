Podcast 1: Agents of Change – How to Optimize for AI Search

Podcast Name: Agents of Change

Episode Title: How to Optimize Your Brand for AI Search with Crystal Carter

Date: February 26, 2025

Host: Rich Brooks (flyte new media)

URL: https://www.theagentsofchange.com/crystal-carter-2

Summary:

Crystal returned to Agents of Change to discuss LLM optimization as a tactical business necessity. She explained why traditional search engine optimization no longer suffices when 3 billion LLM queries per month represent untapped visibility. The conversation covered entity-based optimization, conversational query strategies, and why brands are missing customers by ignoring generative search.


Key Insights:


AI tools understand business entities conceptually; optimize for what your business "is" rather than keyword density

Conversational queries (e.g., "best local specialist for European car repair") replace keyword queries; content must reflect natural language

LLM optimization isn't optional; 25% predicted decline in traditional search by 2026 is real competitive risk

Small businesses can compete by demonstrating genuine expertise and building trusted entity relationships
