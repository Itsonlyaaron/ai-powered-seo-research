Podcast 1 — The Business Owner's Journey with Nick Berry


Podcast Name: The Business Owner's Journey

Episode Title: Matt Diggity: How a World-Class SEO Expert Builds a Digital Marketing Empire

Date: October 2, 2024

Host: Nick Berry

URL: https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/matt-diggity-how-a-world-class-seo-expert-builds/id1747355003?i=1000671527126


Short Summary: Matt traces his path from electrical engineer to building a portfolio of seven-figure SEO businesses, including LeadSpring, The Search Initiative, and The Affiliate Lab. He covers how AI is reshaping SEO strategy, why diversified traffic sources are non-negotiable, and how to hire and retain A-players in a knowledge business.


Key Insights:

-YouTube currently functions as a negative customer acquisition channel for Matt — the platform pays content creators while simultaneously building audience and brand search volume.

-AI in search may ultimately benefit business owners: fewer ads, more direct answers, less competition from intermediaries — if brands are cited correctly.

-Tolerating B or C players destroys company culture from the inside; A-players disengage when they see mediocrity rewarded at the same wage.

-SEO audit and multi-channel diversification (social, video, paid) should precede heavy SEO investment — organic alone is never sufficient infrastructure.






Podcast 2 — Get Yourself Optimized with Stephan Spencer

Podcast Name: Get Yourself Optimized

Episode Title: Finding Inner Calm in a Chaotic World with Matt Diggity (Ep. 544)

Date: December 18, 2025

Host: Stephan Spencer

URL: https://www.getyourselfoptimized.com/finding-inner-calm-in-a-chaotic-world-with-matt-diggity/

Short Summary: Matt opens up about his personal transformation journey — moving beyond pure business success toward inner exploration. He discusses anxiety as a slow-build warning signal, how IFS (Internal Family Systems) therapy rewired his relationship with past trauma, and why somatic practices like resonant breathing and TRE complement the high-performance entrepreneur lifestyle he built.


Key Insights:

-Entrepreneurial success without inner work produces a delayed anxiety crisis — Matt's experience mirrors a pattern common among high-achieving operators who "optimize everything except themselves."

-IFS therapy (parts work) is Matt's recommended modality for dismantling identity-level trauma — the approach treats internal conflict as separate "parts" rather than character flaws.

-Resonant breathing and somatic practices (TRE) offer real-time tension release — critical for founders operating under sustained cognitive and emotional load.

-The "money distraction" pattern — using business growth to avoid addressing personal pain — is a recognized cognitive trap that Matt names explicitly as a phase he had to exit.






Podcast 3 — The Business Owner's Journey (Mini-Series)

Podcast Name: The Business Owner's Journey

Episode Title: Matt Diggity's Hiring Strategy for Identifying and Hiring 'A' Players Only

Date: December 15, 2024

Host: Nick Berry

URL: https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/matt-diggitys-strategy-for-identifying-and-hiring/id1747355003?i=1000680415540

Short Summary: A focused mini-episode drawn from Matt's hiring philosophy across seven SEO businesses. He explains the cultural and financial cost of tolerating underperformers, shares specific interview techniques for identifying A-players, and argues that hiring is the highest-leverage decision in a knowledge business.


Key Insights:

-Matt's preferred interview question — "When I speak to your former boss, what rating will they give you?" — surfaces self-awareness and accountability that scripted answers cannot.

-A single underperformer in a high-performing team is contagious — it signals to A-players that standards are negotiable, triggering disengagement and attrition.

-Partnerships and strategic hiring (not just content or links) are how Matt has generated his largest returns across seven businesses.

-Building high-performing teams is a scalable skill — the same principles that apply in SEO agencies apply across digital businesses and SaaS.
