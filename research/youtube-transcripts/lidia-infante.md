Video 1 — How to Level-Up Content, Tech and Links in Your SEO (SEO in 2024)

Title: How to Level-Up Content, Tech and Links in Your SEO — with Lidia Infante (SEOin2024)

Date: March 11, 2024

Platform: YouTube (Majestic SEO)

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=phkkygmVJJY

Short Summary: In this video (part of the Majestic SEO in 2024 series), Lidia makes the case that AI has fundamentally changed the economics of content creation. Volume is no longer a competitive advantage; differentiation is. She urges SEOs to stop creating content for search volume alone and instead invest in opinionated, expert-driven pieces that genuinely serve their audience.

Key Insights:

-AI has commoditised content production — anyone can generate volume, so volume is no longer a moat.

-Differentiation now requires either best-in-class content quality or a recognisable brand; "table stakes" content loses.

-SEOs should reduce content output and raise quality — e.g., write 5 deeply researched pieces instead of 10 shallow ones.

-AI can be used to interview your audience at scale and extract language and pain points to inform content briefs — not just to generate the content itself.






Video 2 — Headless SEO: I'm Sorry, But This Is Happening (MozCon 2023)

Title: Headless SEO: I'm Sorry, But This Is Happening

Date: Originally presented at MozCon 2023 (Seattle); YouTube upload March 2026 (Moz channel)

Platform: YouTube (Moz)

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AOlWL7mUJ6w

Short Summary: One of Lidia's signature talks, now widely available on YouTube via the Moz channel. She explains the concept of content modelling and why headless CMS architecture is becoming an essential skill for SEOs. The talk demystifies structured content and makes the business case for why SEOs need to stop avoiding engineering conversations.

Key Insights:

-Headless CMS is not just a developer concern — it unlocks SEO capabilities around content velocity, omnichannel distribution, and structured data at scale.

-A single source of truth for content (structured content) frees editors and SEOs to focus on higher-value work.

-SEO is moving "omnichannel" — beyond the 10 blue links to visual search, voice, apps, and rich results.

-SEOs who avoid headless will find themselves excluded from strategic decisions in modern tech stacks.






Video 3 — Put the Human and the Author First to Differentiate Your Content (SEO in 2024 — Majestic)

Title: Put the Human and the Author First to Differentiate Your Content

Date: March 11, 2024

Platform: YouTube / Majestic SEO series

URL: https://majestic.com/seo-in-2024/lidia-infante

Short Summary: Lidia doubles down on the human element in the AI era — arguing that the author's expertise and the reader's genuine needs must be at the centre of any content strategy. She walks through how to combine AI tooling, author expertise, and schema markup to create content that wins in 2024 and beyond. The talk offers practical, implementable 
frameworks rather than vague advice.

Key Insights:

-The combination of AI assistance + genuine author expertise + structured data (schema) is the winning formula for content differentiation.

-Search volumes are a guideline, not a mandate — many valuable searches happen every day that have never been searched before.

-Thin AI content does not just fail to rank — it actively wastes crawl budget and dilutes the authority of genuinely useful pages on the same domain.

-The author's unique perspective and real experience are now ranking signals — E-E-A-T is not just a guideline but a practical competitive advantage.
