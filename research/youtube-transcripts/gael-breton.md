Video 2 — Are Niche Affiliate Sites Dead? | Gael Breton (Authority Hacker)

Title: Are Niche Affiliate Sites Dead? | Gael Breton (Authority Hacker)

Date: June 25, 2024

Platform: YouTube (Ahrefs Podcast channel)

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3s7LWTJLlE4

Short Summary: Breton gives a frank assessment of the post-Helpful Content Update landscape. He explains why pure affiliate SEO is effectively dead for the vast majority of small publishers, while "real business" SEO — for e-commerce, agencies, and services — remains healthy. The conversation also covers community building, topical authority, and how search intent is evolving.


Key Insights:

Affiliate SEO is dead for 99% of small sites; if you have a real product or service, SEO is as strong as ever.

Google is actively rewarding forums, communities, and first-party experience signals over generic review content.

Topical authority requires depth and structure, not volume — thin coverage of many subtopics compounds the risk of deindexing.

Mobile-first optimization and community engagement are the underrated levers most SEOs still ignore.
