Video 1: SEO for Brand Visibility in LLMs

Title: SEO for Brand Visibility in LLMs with Crystal Carter From Wix Studio

Date: September 3, 2025

Platform: YouTube (KP Playbook)

URL: https://kpplaybook.com/resources/seo-for-brand-visibility-in-llms-webinar-recap/

Summary:

Crystal delivered a comprehensive webinar distinguishing between static pre-trained LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini) and dynamic, interactive platforms. She covered monitoring tools, Perplexity Pages optimization, and practical strategies for earned media and journalist citations as leverage points in generative search. The webinar emphasized that 25% search engine use decline is predicted by 2026, with 50% organic traffic decline possible by 2028.


Key Insights:


Two LLM types require different strategies: static models need data inclusion; dynamic models reward active optimization

Perplexity Pages can generate visibility; create topic-specific pages with embedded citations and links

Journalist citations and earned media are critical; brands with news coverage rank better in LLM-generated answers

Smaller brands can access visibility through smaller news outlets and syndicated news collections (e.g., News Corp)





Video 2: Diving into DeepSeek – Generative Search Optimization

Title: Diving into Deepseek Generative Search Optimization

Date: August 7, 2025

Platform: YouTube (iPullRank / SEO Week 2025)

URL: https://ipullrank.com/seo-week-2025-crystal-carter

Summary:

At SEO Week 2025, Crystal broke down DeepSeek's "mixture of experts" (MoE) reasoning model and its implications for content strategy. She demonstrated how reasoning models deliver results by pulling from multiple expertise domains, requiring brands to create nuanced, layered content that demonstrates genuine authority rather than keyword coverage.


Key Insights:


Reasoning models like DeepSeek prioritize multi-disciplinary, layered questions that showcase brand expertise

Content must demonstrate actual knowledge, not just keyword coverage; checklist SEO tactics are obsolete

"Mixture of experts" architecture means brands showing 45% visibility for complex, multifaceted queries demonstrate credibility

AI tools can source topic ideas and research; use them to expand content depth and authority signals





Video 3: SMX Advanced Interview – LLM-SEO Intersection

Title: Crystal Carter on the LLM-SEO intersection, new SEO ideas, and reasoning models

Date: June 3, 2025

Platform: YouTube (Search Engine Land / SMX Advanced)

URL: https://searchengineland.com/crystal-carter-interview-smx-advanced-2025-456460

Summary:

Crystal sat down with Search Engine Land's editorial director to discuss the intersection of LLMs and SEO, emphasizing curiosity and experimentation over outdated playbooks. She outlined how to monitor AI bots (OpenAI crawls more aggressively than Google), the importance of multimodal optimization, and why traditional "checklist SEO" is breaking down.


Key Insights:


SEO industry's strength is curiosity and testing; apply this to rapidly changing AI landscape

OpenAI, Meta, and other AI platforms crawl sites differently than Google; analyze bot behavior patterns

Multimodal presence (video, text, structured data) across multiple channels is critical for future visibility

Playbooks are breaking; the ability to experiment and iterate matters more than following prescriptive tactics
