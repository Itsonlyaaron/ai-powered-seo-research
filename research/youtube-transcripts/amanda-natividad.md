 Title: Zero-Click Content Isn't the Enemy of ROI – It's the Future (Amanda Natividad, SparkToro)
 Date: July 22, 2025
 Platform: YouTube (Databox)
 URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=halAVUDc5F8Short
  
  Summary:
  
   Amanda dives into the now-mainstream concept of "zero-click content," explaining why marketers must stop obsessing over click-through rates. She shares a practical approach to building sustainable impact by optimizing for native platform consumption, impressions, and brand affinity.  
  
  Key Insights:
  
  Zero-click content natively delivers value without requiring the user to leave the platform, which algorithmically boosts reach.  Marketers need to transition from measuring direct ROI to tracking VOI (Value on Investment).  Stakeholder alignment is critical, teams must map zero-click metrics to long-term business objectives.Content should be heavily repurposed across different channels to fuel multiple teams efficiently.

 Title: The Future of Marketing in an Algorithm-Driven World - Amanda Natividad
 Date: 2025
 Platform: YouTube (orenmeetsworld)
 URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kO5JXEwQ6LMShort 
 
 Summary:
 
  In this extensive interview, Amanda discusses how to operate as a "one-person marketing machine" in a landscape dominated by algorithms that throttle external links. She explores ways to gain traction when traditional marketing funnels are losing their effectiveness.
 
 Key Insights:
 
 Earning attention on an algorithm-driven, traffic-starved web requires giving the best insights away for free natively.Building an owned audience (like an email list) is the ultimate safeguard against algorithmic shifts.Personal branding and unique point-of-views are essential for standing out in crowded B2B spaces.
