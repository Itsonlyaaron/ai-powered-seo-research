Video 1 — "SEO in the Age of AI" | Page 2 Podcast
Title: SEO in the Age of AI 🤖 | Kevin Indig on Google Overviews, E-Commerce & The Future of Search
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qujABKOAThA
Date: September 15, 2025
Platform: YouTube / Page 2 Podcast (hosted by Jon Clark and Joe DeVita)

Main Insights:

Indig argues AI Overviews are already compressing click-through rates, backed by a meta-analysis he conducted across 19 studies — all of which found negative CTR impact.
He introduces the "query fan-out" concept: AI Mode doesn't respond to a single query but expands it into multiple related sub-queries and stitches the answers together, meaning brands need comprehensive topic coverage, not just one optimised page.
His UX study (70 users, 8 tasks, 13,000 annotations, 30 hours of video) found that only ~20% of users stop at the AI Overview — 80% still click through to blue links, YouTube, maps, or shopping results, which is more encouraging than the industry feared.
Older demographics distrust AI answers and go straight to traditional results, creating a meaningful audience segment that still values classic SEO.
Key recommendation: add clear "key takeaway" navigation boxes to content pages; his study showed users bounced much faster when they had to hunt for the key answer within long-form articles.
