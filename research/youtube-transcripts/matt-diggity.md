Video 3 — How to Rank on AI Search / GEO vs SEO

Title: What is GEO? How to Rank on AI Search Engines in 2025 (GEO vs SEO Explained)

Date: Referenced and discussed by Matt in May–June 2025 posts and X threads

Platform: YouTube / Webinar

URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4G3cDjShmZA

Short Summary: Matt has actively amplified and synthesized the emerging discipline of Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — the practice of optimizing content to appear in AI-generated responses from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. He outlined how GEO differs from traditional SEO and what the highest-leverage ranking factors are in AI-answer engines.


Key Insights:

-GEO requires optimizing for AI citation logic, not keyword density — AI systems favor independently cited, editorially referenced brands and entities.

-Top SEOs surviving the AI shift are treating their brand's web footprint as the primary optimization surface, not individual page authority.

-AI search engines do not follow Google's ranking rules — authority before visibility, nofollow links contributing to trust, and image-based backlinks outperforming text links are all observed GEO phenomena.

-The transition from "rank in Google" to "get cited by AI" is the defining strategic pivot of 2025–2026.
