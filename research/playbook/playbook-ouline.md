# AI-Powered SEO Playbook (Draft)

This playbook is derived from research conducted across ten AI-powered SEO practitioners.

## Core Themes

- AI Search Optimization
- Content Quality & Topical Authority
- Zero-Click Marketing
- Semantic SEO
- Technical SEO
- Content Distribution
- Brand Visibility
- AI-Assisted Content Production

## Common Patterns

- AI is changing search behaviour.
- Brands are becoming more important than rankings.
- Topical authority consistently outperforms isolated keyword targeting.
- Human expertise remains essential when using AI.
- Content distribution is as important as content creation.

## Future Work

This repository will be expanded into a complete AI-powered SEO playbook by synthesizing recurring frameworks and best practices from all researched experts.
