amanda natividad - Summary
Amanda Natividad is the Chief Evangelist at audience research startup SparkToro and the founder of the Zero Click Marketing consultancy and podcast. A former tech journalist, Le Cordon Bleu-trained chef, and experienced B2B marketing leader, she is highly relevant to AI-powered SEO and B2B SaaS because she pioneered the concept of "zero-click content"—teaching marketers how to thrive in an era where platforms and AI engines actively suppress outbound traffic



Main Themes
- Zero-Click Content: The necessity of providing complete, native value within social feeds and search platforms without demanding a click-through.

- Rethinking Measurement: Shifting the focus from direct attribution (ROI and clicks) to broader metrics like reach, impressions, brand affinity, and Value on Investment (VOI). 

- Human Authenticity in an AI World: Leveraging lived experience, personal anecdotes, and emotional intelligence as the primary differentiators against AI-generated content.  




AI & Content Marketing Insights 
- AI visibility should be viewed as a mirror reflecting the actual strength of your brand's digital footprint, not just a score to be gamed. 

- AI can aggregate information and predict experiences, but it cannot organically replicate human "lived experience."  

- As web traffic becomes increasingly scarce due to AI answers and platform algorithms, marketing tech stacks will shrink, forcing every tool to definitively prove its value.Gating content is becoming less effective.

- marketers should ungate the utility/argument to maximize reach and build trust natively.B2B purchasing decisions carry high emotional stakes (e.g., job security), requiring marketing that offers deep reassurance and trust, rather than just listing software features.
